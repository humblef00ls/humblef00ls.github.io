Sky_Vault



    

       Sky_Vault is the first back end web app I ever made. It is a simple and free text archiving platform, where you
        can upload/type/paste any text up to 2000 characters, and assign it a password/key, which is what then further
        encrypts your stored text.

 I really was just bored one day and thought this would be cool way to archive and share
        texts.


        The whole backend was made in PHP7, and Sky_Vault2.0 will be coming soon [In Node.JS this time!].
